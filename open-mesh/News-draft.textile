h1. DRAFT: Batman-adv 2017.1 released

May 15th, 2017. Today the B.A.T.M.A.N. team publishes the May 2017 update to batman-adv, batctl and alfred! This release XXXXXX. Also several bugfixes and code cleanups are included in this version.
